How Our AI Detection System Works

1

Content Analysis

Our AI analyzes job descriptions using machine learning models trained on thousands of legitimate and fraudulent job postings to identify suspicious patterns.

2

Domain Verification

We perform WHOIS lookups to check domain age, registration details, and SSL certificate status to verify company legitimacy.

3

Email Authentication

Email domains are cross-referenced with company websites to detect mismatches and identify free email providers commonly used in scams.

4

Risk Scoring

All factors are combined using our proprietary algorithm to generate a comprehensive risk score and detailed recommendations.
