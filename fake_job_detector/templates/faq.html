FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

How accurate is the AI detection?

Our ML model is trained on thousands of job postings with 85%+ accuracy. It combines rule-based detection with machine learning for comprehensive analysis.

What is the risk score?

Risk score ranges from 0-100. Low (0-35), Medium (35-65), High (65-100). It's calculated based on suspicious keywords, domain age, SSL, email verification, and ML prediction.

Is my data safe?

Yes! We don't store personal information. Only job descriptions and company names are saved for analysis history. No payment or sensitive data is collected.

Can I report a fake company?

Absolutely! Visit the Fake Companies page and use the complaint submission form. Your report helps protect others.

How do I download reports?

After analyzing a job, click "Download PDF Report" button. You can also export your entire scan history as CSV from the History page.

What are red flags?

Red flags are suspicious keywords like "urgent hiring", "registration fee", "guaranteed income", "gmail contact", etc. that commonly appear in fake job postings.

Can I trust a low-risk job?

Low risk means fewer red flags detected, but always verify through official company channels. Never pay fees for job applications.
